How do I get rides from Sawari?

In order to use the service as a client, one has to download the Sawari app. You can book a ride in just 2 taps. Select your pick-up and drop-off locations and confirm the ride with an estimated fare shown aside. Sawari will find you the available driver and you can know your driver’s location and estimated time of arrival in real time. The request for rides can only be made through the application. We have no provision of matching the client with a Sawari partner by any other means. Also, please make sure you have an internet connection when you make a booking.

How do I give rides to earn money?

You have to become a Sawari partner to give rides and earn money.To become a Sawari partner, download the Sawari Partner app from Playstore, sign up with your phone number & upload the required documents. You can also sign up from the website. Please bring the following documents during your training session: Valid License (professional/non-professional), Valid Bluebook, Valid Registration paper. For more details, please attend the training sessions. The link to Safari partner app is:

Can I use the same mobile number to use both Sawari and Sawari partner apps?

Yes, you can use same mobile number to use both the app. However, the process to sign up is different. You will have to sign up on both the apps separately using the process mentioned above. If you have signed up only in the Sawari app, do not expect the same account to work in the Sawari partner app. You have to sign up again for the Sawari partner app following the required procedures even if you use the same mobile number.

Do I have to turn on Mobile data throughout my journey?

For clients, you have to turn on the mobile data or have an internet connection till the driver accepts your ride request. After that it is optional. However, if you still want to turn on your mobile data throughout the ride, you get some additional features like knowing the driver status and tracking the driver on the way. For the driver, yes you will have to turn on your Mobile data throughout the ride. PS: The data cost for using our application for one particular ride of about 5 km is Rs 1. Make sure you have turned off other applications from using your mobile data at that particular time. You can change this in your settings.

What if my rider drove dangerously/behaved rudely?

For this type of issue, you may ask the rider for slowing down bike speed or you can ask him to park in any safe place then call us immediately or call us at 9810477387. After sharing with us about this issue we will take corrective measures for the rider. Your safety is our first concern.

Why is my account suspended? How to appeal?

Please call us at our helpline to know the suspension reason and duration. You have to visit our Walk-In-Support center to withdraw your suspension.
